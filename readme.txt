The official LIEU.city plugin for WordPress

== Description ==

The user, through the WordPress plugin can, after creating his exhibitions VR on the platform LIEU. city, will show its exhibitions and its windows virtual on the WordPress site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is it necessary to have a LIEU account to use the plugin? =

Yes, it is necessary to have a LIEU account, which will be created free of charge at [www.lieu.city](https://www.lieu.city).

= Why can't I see my exhibitions? =

Make sure your exhibitions are published on LIEU.city.

= what can I do if the plugin does not work? =

Try to log again or contact LIEU.city customer service at <lieu@lieu.city>.
